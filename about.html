Our story

Dallas's Premier Finish Carpentry Team

Our mission is to enhance homes through meticulous craftsmanship, personalized service, and a commitment to excellence.

Finish Carpentry LLC was founded on the belief that every home deserves flawless woodwork. Starting as a small crew serving the Lakewood neighborhood, we've grown into one of Dallas's most trusted finish carpentry companies. Our founder, a master carpenter with over 15 years of experience, instilled a culture of precision, honesty, and pride in every project.

Today, we serve homeowners across the Dallas metroplex, from historic M Streets to modern Preston Hollow. We specialize in crown molding, custom built-ins, and trim work that transforms ordinary rooms into extraordinary spaces. Our 5.0 rating reflects our unwavering dedication to quality and customer satisfaction.
