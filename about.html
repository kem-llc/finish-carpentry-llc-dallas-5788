Our story

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

To provide Dallas homeowners with exceptional custom carpentry that enhances their living spaces and stands the test of time.

Finish Carpentry LLC was founded on a simple belief: quality workmanship never goes out of style. With over 15 years of experience in the Dallas area, our founder started the company to bring a higher standard of finish carpentry to local homeowners. We take pride in every project, no matter the size.

From our first custom built-in to our latest crown molding installation, we have built a reputation for reliability, precision, and beautiful results. Our team treats your home with the same care we would our own, and we are committed to exceeding expectations every time.
