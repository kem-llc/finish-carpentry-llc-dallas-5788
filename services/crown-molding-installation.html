Crown Molding Installation

Elegant crown molding that adds architectural interest and value to any room.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our crown molding services include custom profiles, multi-tier designs, and seamless coped corners. We work with both primed wood and polyurethane materials to match your style and budget.

What's included

  • Enhances room aesthetics and home value
  • Hides imperfections at wall-ceiling joints
  • Customizable to any architectural style

How it works

1

Consultation

We visit your home, discuss design options, and take precise measurements.

2

Fabrication

We cut and cope molding in our workshop for perfect, gap-free joints.

3

Installation

We install the molding with professional-grade adhesives and nails, then caulk and touch up paint.

Common questions

How long does crown molding installation take?

Most rooms take 1-2 days, depending on complexity and room size.

Do you paint the molding?

We install primed molding and can paint it to match your walls, or you can paint it after installation.
